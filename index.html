From the club's inception in 2005 to the present day, Football Club Wycombe has a rich history. FC Wycombe, often known as Wycombe and Buckinghamshire, is a football club headquartered in Wycombe, England. Azam Azher gathered a collection of Swiss, English, German, and Spanish footballers to form the team in 2007. Until 2020, the club competed in amateur football tournaments in the region. The club competed in their first European competition in June 2012, winning one UEFA trophy. In the year 2016, Wycombe has never been relegated from La Liga as of 2020, a record shared by Athletic Bilbao and arch-rival Real Madrid.


Our goal is to utilize football as a tool to assist F.C. Wycombe flourish; to work with the commercial and governmental sectors to improve the quality of football at all levels; to improve the football experience for fans; and to convert football into a successful business for all parties concerned (owners, players, media, & officials).
